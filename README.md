## 👋 About Us

Welcome to **Hani Ladies Designer Studio** — a dedicated learning center for women passionate about fashion design, embroidery, and tailoring. Founded with a mission to preserve and celebrate traditional craftsmanship while building professional skills, we offer comprehensive courses in:

- **Aari Hand Embroidery** - Master the delicate art of hand-stitched embellishments
- **Machine Embroidery** - Learn modern embroidery techniques with precision
- **Tailoring & Stitching** - Transform fabrics into beautifully fitted garments
- **Overall Ladies Designer** - Comprehensive design and construction training

## 🎓 What We Do

We believe every person deserves the opportunity to learn valuable skills that can transform their life. Through personalized, hands-on instruction, we help our students:

✨ Develop professional expertise in garment design and construction  
✨ Build confidence through practical, real-world projects  
✨ Create a supportive community of passionate learners  
✨ Unlock income generation opportunities through creative skills  

Our instructor, **Krishnaveni**, is a **DST Certified Trainer** with 15+ years of experience in ladies' garment designing and education. She brings not just technical expertise, but genuine passion for nurturing the next generation of designers.
