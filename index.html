About Us

10+ Years Experience | DST Certified
Hello, I am Krishnaveni "I am a DST Certified Trainer (RSETI) specializing in both Aari Hand Embroidery and Tailoring. With over 15 years of experience in ladies' garment designing and teaching at various institutions since 2012, I ensure every student masters the art perfectly."

Welcome to Hani Ladies Designer Studio. We specialize in professional ladies designer training and custom designing. Our goal is to empower women through skill development.

We provide home classes with personal attention to ensure every student masters the art of embroidery and tailoring. Whether you are a beginner or looking to upgrade your skills, we have the right course for you.
